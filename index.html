Property Overview

  • Luxury Living That’s Ready and Waiting

    MNM MB Elegant is a premium residential project that redefines luxury living in the heart of KR Puram, Bangalore. Strategically located in Medahalli, this exclusive enclave of ready-to-move-in 3 BHK apartments blends contemporary architecture with functional design to offer you the comfort and convenience your family deserves.

    Built by MNM Builders, a name known for quality and trust, MB Elegant is thoughtfully designed to deliver a high standard of urban living while ensuring peace and privacy. Each home is spacious, well-ventilated, and finished with top-class materials, creating the perfect ambiance for modern families.

    Approved by BBMP and equipped with an E-Khata, the project ensures a transparent and secure investment. With prices starting from just ₹81 lakhs, MNM MB Elegant is not just a home—it’s a lifestyle choice.

    Whether you’re looking to upgrade your living or make a smart real estate investment, MNM MB Elegant is where sophistication meets affordability.

SPECIFICATIONS

FRAMED STRUCTURE

RCC framed structure

SUPER STRUCTURE

6" Solid concrete blocks for external walls
4" Solid concrete blocks for internal walls
No common walls.

PLASTERING

Internal wall: 1:5 C.M. with Cement rendering smooth finish.
External wall: smooth sponge finish cement plastering.

FLOORING

2' x 2' Vitrified tiles for Living, dining, kitchen and all bedrooms with 4" skirting.
Ceramic Anti-skid tiles for Utilities & Balconies.

DOORS

Main Door: Teak wood frames with Teak wood shutters and brass fittings.
Internal Doors: Sal wood frames for bedrooms with Flush door shutters and necessary fittings.

WINDOWS

Three Track 90 series UPVC windows with mosquito mesh and M.S. Grills with enamel paint.

KITCHEN

Granite kitchen platform with Stainless steel Sink and 2 feet height glazed tiles dadoing above the platform.
Provision for Washing machine & Dishwasher.
Provision for water purifier, Chimney and electrical hub.

CAR PARKING

Covered car parking. Separate space allocated for each flat.

TOILETS

8 feet height Vitrified tile (2' x 4') dadoing & Anti-skid tiles flooring.
European commode, wash basin with tap fitting and taps (Parryware / Cera / Similar).

ELECTRICAL

Concealed copper wiring (Anchor / similar wire) with Anchor/similar plate switches & necessary points in each room.
15 amps power plug points in Kitchen and Toilets, ELCB (Earth leaking circuit breakers).
Individual TV & Telephone points in Living Hall & Master bedroom.
AC point for all three bedrooms.

PAINTING

Inside: Two coats of Putty further added on with one coat of Primer and two coats of Asian tractor Emulsion paint.
Outside: one coat of primer with two coats Asian Ace paints, Enamel paint for doors and window grills.

WATER SUPPLY

24 hrs water deep Tube well, Sump & Over head tank.
Provision for Cauvery connection. Separate Cauvery water line provided in each unit kitchen.

LOBBY & LIFT

Luxuriously designed entrance lobby with premium finishes. Spacious lifts with high-quality interiors and anti-slip flooring.
2 high-speed elevators with emergency backup.

GENERATOR

Standby power generator for common areas and emergency lighting. 100% power backup for lifts, water pumps, and common lighting.

FIRE SAFETY

Fire detection and alarm system in each floor.
Fire extinguishers in common areas and basements.
Sprinkler system in all floors and car parking areas.

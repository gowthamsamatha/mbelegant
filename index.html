SPECIFICATIONS

STRUCTURE

SRCC framed structure with seismic compliance resistance.

SUPER STRUCTURE

6” Solid blocks/Light Weight for external walls.
4” Solid blocks/Light Weight for internal walls.
No common walls.

WINDOWS

UPVC windows with mosquito mesh provision & safety MS grills for all windows.

FLOORING

Vitrified tiles of premium quality for living area, bedroom & kitchen.
Antiskid tiles for toilets, balconies, & utilities.

WATER SUPPLY

24 hrs water deep tube well, sump & over head tank.

KITCHEN

Granite kitchen platform with stainless steel sink.
2 feet height glazed tiles dado above the platform.
Provision for washing machine, water purifier, chimney and electrical hub.

TOILET

Ceramic glazed wall tiles and ceramic flooring.

SANITARY WARE

European commode, washbasin, CP fittings and diverters for all toilets of parryware/Soncerra/Jaquar or equivalent.

PLASTERING

Internal wall: 1:5 C.M. with lime rendering smooth finish.
External wall: smooth sponge finish cement plastering.

PAINTING

Inside: One coat of primer with two coats tractor emulsion paints.
Outside: One coat of primer & two coats Asian apex or equivalent paints.
Main Door: Teak polish and enamel paint for other doors and windows.

DOORS

Main Door: Teak wood frame with teak wood shutter.
Other Door: Saal wood frame with moulded skin doors with standard hardware fittings.

LOCKS

Godrej locks or equivalent for all doors.

ELECTRICAL

Concealed copper wiring (Anchor or equivalent) with necessary points in each room.
15 amps power plug points in kitchen and toilets.
Individual TV & telephone points in main hall & master bedroom, AC points.

LIFT & LOBBY

Entrance lobby finished with granite flooring staircase with railings.
Two lifts of Kone/Johnson or equivalent of 6 passenger capacity with ARD system.

CAR PARKING

One car parking for each flat.

POWER BACK-UP

Power back-up for each flat and additional power back-up for lifts, water pumps & common area lighting.
